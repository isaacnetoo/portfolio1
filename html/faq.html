Frequently Asked Questions

Is the flight ticket included in the trek package?

Yes, the flight ticket is generally included in the trek package cost. However, it's essential to confirm this with the trek organizer or tour agency before booking.

What are the luggage restrictions for the trekking flight?

Luggage restrictions can vary based on the airline and the specific trek. Generally, for domestic flights to trekking destinations, there might be restrictions on both checked and carry-on luggage. It's advisable to check with the airline for precise details on luggage allowances and restrictions.
